Hi, I'm Paige.



I have always been a visual artist, but I am now bringing my experience with physical objects to web design. Most of my work involves 3-dimensional books that are meant to be interacted with, and they always include goofy monsters and robots. I think someone should always enjoy their engagement with a product, whether that is a hand-bound book or a web app.

My background also includes linguistics, teaching, and copy writing/editing. Text, images, and form should come together to create an intuitive and enjoyable subconscious learning experience.
